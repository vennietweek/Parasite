A Pillow Made by the World Pickiest Designer

Those who suffer from a restless sleep often report, “I wake up several times at night” or “I wake up feeling tired.” These common sleeping issues, we discovered, are a result of the hampered sideway rotations of your head while you sleep. Kitamura Makura thus asked, “How can we facilitate smooth rotations of the head?” This inquiry is what led to the creation of KM05. In crafting such a pillow, we have incorporated the experience and knowledge from the decade-long lessons of our gymnast series, while at the same time we have reconsidered our well-established method of sewing, the structure of pillow, inner materials, and texture, as well as the aesthetic of the overall shape. The result is a pillow that is comfortable for the head to rest on and beautiful for the eye to look at. KM05 is born.
